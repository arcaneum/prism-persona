# Persona Conflicts

Contradictions, tensions, or uncertainties that require human review before resolution.

---

## Status Key

- **PENDING REVIEW** — detected, awaiting review
- **RESOLVED** — reviewed and decided
- **DISMISSED** — not a real conflict

## Resolution Options

- **UPDATE** — replace old position with new
- **KEEP ORIGINAL** — original position stands
- **NUANCE** — add context to accommodate both positions
