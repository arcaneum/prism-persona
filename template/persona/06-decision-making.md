# 06 — Decision-Making

> Risk tolerance, research depth, and the frameworks you use to make choices. This helps an AI system reason the way you would when faced with trade-offs.

---

## Guiding Questions

- How thoroughly do you research before making decisions?
- What's your risk tolerance? Do you move fast or deliberate?
- Do you use specific frameworks for decision-making?
- How do you handle decisions with incomplete information?
- What factors do you weigh most heavily (cost, quality, speed, ethics)?
- How do you document and communicate decisions?

## Structure Guide

Describe your default approach, then note how it changes under different conditions (time pressure, high stakes, reversible vs. irreversible decisions). If you use named frameworks (e.g., RICE, Eisenhower matrix, decision records), list them and explain when each applies.
