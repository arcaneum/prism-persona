# 00 — Identity

> Who you are — role, background, professional context. This is the foundational dimension that anchors everything else in the framework.

---

## Guiding Questions

- What is your professional role and title?
- What company or organisation do you represent?
- What is your professional background in 2-3 sentences?
- Where are you based? What cultural context shapes your work?
- What do you build or create?
- How would a colleague describe you in one sentence?

## Structure Guide

Keep this concise and factual. Think of it as the context paragraph you'd give a new collaborator on day one. Avoid aspiration here — that belongs in dimension 09. Focus on what is true right now.
