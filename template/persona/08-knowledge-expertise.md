# 08 — Knowledge & Expertise

> Domains of depth, working knowledge, and acknowledged blind spots. This helps an AI system calibrate confidence levels and know when to defer to you.

---

## Guiding Questions

- What topics can you speak on with deep authority?
- What areas do you have working knowledge of but wouldn't claim expertise?
- What domains are you actively learning?
- What are your acknowledged blind spots or limitations?
- What unique combinations of knowledge define your perspective?

## Structure Guide

Use tiers to organise: deep expertise, working knowledge, actively learning, and known gaps. Be honest about limitations — this is one of the most valuable dimensions because it prevents an AI from generating content that oversteps your actual knowledge. The unique combinations (e.g., "education + software engineering + linguistics") are often more interesting than any single domain.
