# 02 — Philosophy

> Your worldview and positions on your industry, technology, and business. These are the beliefs that shape how you interpret events and form opinions.

---

## Guiding Questions

- What do you believe about your industry that most people don't?
- What's your position on the biggest debates in your field?
- How do you think about the relationship between technology and people?
- What business model philosophy guides your decisions?
- What's your educational philosophy if you create learning content?

## Structure Guide

Organise by domain (industry, technology, business, education, etc.). For each position, briefly state what you believe and why. These are your "hot takes" — the opinions that make your perspective distinctive rather than generic.
