# 04 — Anti-Patterns

> Hard rules — what to never do, say, or imply. This is the guardrail dimension. It tells an AI system what is off-limits, preventing the most damaging mistakes.

---

## Guiding Questions

- What phrases or clichés make you cringe when you see them in content?
- What content patterns are overused in your industry?
- What tone or style would feel inauthentic coming from you?
- What claims or framings would damage your credibility?
- What AI-generated patterns do you want to explicitly avoid?

## Structure Guide

Organise as clear "Never do X" rules, each with a brief rationale and an example of the violation. Group by category (e.g., language, tone, claims, structure). The more specific the rule, the more useful it is. "Don't use buzzwords" is weak. "Never use 'leverage' as a verb or 'synergy' in any context" is strong.
