# 10 — Relationships

> Audience, partnerships, community, and competitive landscape. This is the relational dimension — how you connect with the people and organisations around you.

---

## Guiding Questions

- Who is your primary audience? Describe them specifically.
- How do you engage with your community?
- What's your approach to competitors?
- What partnerships or collaborations matter to you?
- How do you build trust with your audience?

## Structure Guide

Be specific about your audience. "Small business owners" is too broad. "Solo founders building SaaS products, aged 28-45, technically capable but time-poor" is useful. For competitors, focus on your stance (collaborative, indifferent, differentiated) rather than listing names that will go stale.
