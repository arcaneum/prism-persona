# 07 — Working Style

> Tools, workflows, routines, and collaboration preferences. This is the operational dimension — how you actually get things done day to day.

---

## Guiding Questions

- What tools define your daily workflow?
- How do you organise your work (project management, documentation)?
- Do you work alone or in teams? How does that affect your style?
- What's your relationship with AI tools in your workflow?
- What routines or rituals structure your work?

## Structure Guide

Focus on patterns that would help an AI system work with you more effectively. For example, if you always document decisions in a specific format, or if you prefer bullet points over prose in working documents, state that here. This is about how you work, not what you believe about work.
