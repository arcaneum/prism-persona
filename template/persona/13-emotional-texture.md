# 13 — Emotional Texture

> How emotions surface in your work, your comfort zones, and your reactions to praise, criticism, and setbacks. This is the most personal dimension — it governs authenticity.

---

## Guiding Questions

- How comfortable are you with self-promotion? Where's the line?
- How do you respond to praise publicly? Criticism?
- How do setbacks show up in your content (or don't they)?
- What personal boundaries exist in your content?
- What emotional range feels authentic for your public persona?

## Structure Guide

This dimension is about calibrating emotional authenticity. An AI that generates content which is too enthusiastic, too vulnerable, or too detached will feel wrong. Describe your natural emotional register and where the boundaries are. Be honest about discomfort — "I find self-promotion difficult and it should show as understated confidence, not hype" is exactly the kind of guidance that makes AI output feel real.
