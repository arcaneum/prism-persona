# 11 — Content Creation

> Formats, platforms, quality standards, and publishing cadence. This is the operational side of your public output — what you make, where it goes, and how good it needs to be.

---

## Guiding Questions

- What platforms do you publish on?
- What content formats do you use most?
- What are your non-negotiable quality standards?
- How often do you publish? Is cadence important or quality-driven?
- What tools and workflows power your content creation?

## Structure Guide

List your active platforms and the content types for each. Define quality gates — the checks content must pass before publishing. If cadence matters, state your target rhythm. If you publish only when something is ready, say that. This dimension pairs closely with Voice & Tone (03) and Anti-Patterns (04).
