# 03 — Voice & Tone

> Writing style, rhythm, and sentence patterns. This is how you sound on the page — the cadence and texture that makes your writing recognisably yours.

---

## Guiding Questions

- How would you describe your writing voice in 3 adjectives?
- What does your writing sound like when read aloud? Conversational? Academic? Direct?
- What sentence lengths feel natural to you? Short and punchy, or flowing and analytical?
- How do you open pieces? With a story, a fact, a question?
- How do you typically end pieces?

## Structure Guide

If you have an existing voice or style guide, link to it here rather than duplicating. Otherwise, describe your voice with concrete examples: "I write like this, not like that." Include rhythm patterns (e.g., "I favour short declarative sentences followed by one longer analytical sentence").
