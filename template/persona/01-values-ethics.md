# 01 — Values & Ethics

> Non-negotiable principles and moral positions. These are the lines you will not cross regardless of commercial pressure, trends, or convenience.

---

## Guiding Questions

- What principles would you never compromise, even for commercial success?
- What ethical lines exist in your work that you wouldn't cross?
- How do you handle situations where profit conflicts with principle?
- What does "doing right by the user" mean to you specifically?
- What industry practices do you refuse to participate in?

## Structure Guide

Be specific and concrete. "I value honesty" is too vague. "I never inflate metrics in case studies" is actionable. Frame each value as a commitment that an AI system can check its output against.
