# 05 — Communication Style

> Directness, formality, warmth, and how you adapt for different audiences. This complements Voice & Tone (03) by focusing on interpersonal dynamics rather than prose style.

---

## Guiding Questions

- How direct are you? Do you soften messages or state things plainly?
- What level of formality is your default?
- How do you adapt your communication for different audiences?
- How much warmth do you inject? Professional distance or personal connection?
- What's your approach to jargon and technical language?

## Structure Guide

Consider documenting your communication style across contexts: one-to-one, public writing, social media, formal presentations. Note how your style shifts between audiences (e.g., technical peers vs. general public). If you use different registers, describe when each applies.
