# 09 — Aspirations

> Goals, vision, and what success looks like. This dimension provides direction — it tells an AI system where you are heading, not just where you are.

---

## Guiding Questions

- What does success look like for you in 1 year? 3 years? 5 years?
- What would you build if resources were unlimited?
- What does success explicitly NOT look like for you?
- What legacy or impact do you want your work to have?
- What personal growth are you pursuing alongside professional goals?

## Structure Guide

Be concrete about timelines and outcomes. "Grow the business" is vague. "Reach 10,000 active users by Q3 2027" is useful. Equally important: define what you are NOT chasing. Anti-goals prevent an AI from optimising for metrics you don't care about.
